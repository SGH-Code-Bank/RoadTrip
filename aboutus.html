~Our Mission~

To offer users a convenient way to personalize their road trips.

Our team has worked on this web app throughout the course of
CSC 3380 (Object Oriented Design). Trying to organize a road trip
to your favorite vacation spot can be a huge hassle. Something that
can be even more difficult is searching and planning for activities
without going over your budget. As a team, we aim to solve the
problem by offering users a quick, easy way to plan out their trip,
find entertaining stops along their route, and help them budget
their money. The paths we had to take to deliver a promising final
product were great learning experiences. We take the long way to
give users a shortcut to a personalize their experience.
